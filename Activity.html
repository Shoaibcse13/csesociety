Our Activity


Cse Carnival

Arranging a nation wide carnival is an annual event for CSE Society. Programmers and Developers from all over the country participate in our Carnival. It is considered to be one the biggest tech arrangement in the country.

National Tour

CSE Society believes that a sound mind generate sound ideas. Thats why it arranges national tour every year. By this arrangement future CS grads and tech. people of Bangladesh, studying in SUST can introduce themselves to the natural beauties and heritages of this Nation

Picnic

While the tough schedule of self development and course activities is conducted in the department students need relaxation. That is when CSEngland society arrange picnics. Which provides a day of full refreshment and relaxation. Students and teachers become energized again to pursue their academic and extra curriculur activities again.

Workshop

For continuous development of the Grad student at various fields CSE society arranges workshops on different topics life Web development, android development, Algorithmic programming, Machine learning so on and so forth. Thus SUST CSE students acquire knowledge and experience from the experts of the department.

Seminar

We all know that, "To be a master, follow the master". CSE Society SUST bring many prominent tech. speakers from all over the country to the CSE Dept, SUST and arrange seminars with them. The students, thus get the opportunity to learn from the prominent tech. persons and excel in their own field.
